Privacy Policy for Goalpost

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Goalpost ("the App") is a personal savings and goal tracking app developed by Christy Lam. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. The App has no servers and no analytics.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into the App — including goals, savings amounts, income, expenses, and any photos you attach — is stored locally on your device and synchronized via Apple's iCloud service to your other devices signed into the same Apple ID. We do not have access to this data. Apple's privacy policy governs the iCloud sync: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Third Parties

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
