Frequently Asked Questions

  • My data isn't syncing across devices. Make sure both devices are signed into the same iCloud account and have iCloud Drive enabled in Settings. Sync may take a few minutes after you make changes.
  • I lost my data after reinstalling the app. Your data syncs from iCloud on first launch. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID on the device. Initial sync can take a few minutes — please be patient.
  • How do I delete all my data? Deleting the app removes the local copy. To remove your iCloud copy as well, go to Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Goalpost → Delete.
  • Can I export my data? Not yet — but we're working on it. Email us if this is important to you.
  • Will Goalpost work without internet? Yes. The app works fully offline. Your changes will sync to iCloud the next time you have a connection.
